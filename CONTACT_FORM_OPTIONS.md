# Contact Form Integration Options

## Option 1: Google Form (What you asked for) ✅

### Setup Steps:

1. **Create Google Form:**
   - Go to https://forms.google.com
   - Click "Blank form"
   - Add these fields:
     - Name (Short answer, required)
     - Email (Short answer, required)
     - Message (Paragraph, required)

2. **Get Embed URL:**
   - Click "Send" (top right)
   - Click "</>" (embed icon)
   - Copy the iframe `src` URL (looks like: `https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/.../viewform?embedded=true`)

3. **Share the URL with me** and I'll replace the current form with the Google Form embed.

### Pros:
- ✅ Responses go directly to Google Sheets
- ✅ Free
- ✅ Easy to manage responses

### Cons:
- ⚠️ Will have Google's default styling (doesn't match your portfolio design)
- ⚠️ Less customizable
